### About Me
I am an **Assistant Professor** at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, **Premier University, Chittagong (PUC)**. My primary research interests lie at the intersection of **Computer Vision** and **Deep Learning**.

I completed my **M.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering** from Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET) under the supervision of **Dr. Kaushik Deb**. Previously, I earned my **B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering** from Premier University, Chittagong.

### Research Interests
- Computer Vision  
- Deep Learning & Neural Networks  
- Image Processing  
- Medical Image Analysis  
- Generative AI
